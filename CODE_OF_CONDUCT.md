# Code of Conduct

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, precise, and constructive.
- Focus on technical facts and reproducible evidence.
- Keep disagreements about code and design, not people.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated low-signal spam or intentionally disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content and may block accounts for repeated or severe violations.

## Reporting

For conduct issues, open a private security advisory and label it `conduct`.
